e-Krishok: Farmer’s Knowledge Network

BIID has taken initiative bring the benefits of ICT to the farmers community in different location in Bangladesh through e-Krishok, an ICT based knowledge sharing network of farmers. It is evident that limited access of farmers to relevant information and services makes them suffer productivity constraints and vulnerable to crop related diseases. Also farmers lack marketing and input related issues, which will be addressed by e-Krishok initiative. More...
